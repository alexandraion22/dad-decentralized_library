Cambridge professor Dr. David Weston has always been content to study the past rather than experience adventure himself. But when he discovers a mysterious medieval manuscript that appears to detail a lost Byzantine treasure, he finds himself drawn into an unexpected quest. His search takes him across Europe and the Middle East, where he encounters descendants of ancient families, decodes architectural clues in forgotten churches, and gradually realizes he's not the only one hunting the treasure. As present dangers begin to mirror historical conflicts, David must use his knowledge of the past to navigate the complexities of the modern world and discover what truly matters on his own historian's journey.
