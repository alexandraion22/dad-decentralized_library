The old Victorian house on Blackwood Hill has stood empty for decades, its overgrown garden a tangle of thorns and forgotten flowers. When recently widowed Eliza Blackwood inherits the property from a distant relative, she sees it as a chance to start anew. But as she begins to restore the neglected gardens, she uncovers disturbing secrets buried among the roots. Strange shadows move between the trees at night, and the plants seem to grow with unnatural speed. Soon Eliza realizes that something in the garden is watching her, something that has waited patiently in the shadows for someone to return to the house.
