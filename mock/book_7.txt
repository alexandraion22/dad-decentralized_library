After a devastating breakup, struggling artist Claire Morgan retreats to a small coastal town to heal and focus on her painting. She rents a charming cottage by the sea, unaware that its owner, reclusive novelist James Thornton, lives in the larger house just up the hill. Their initial encounters are tense and awkward, but as a severe winter storm isolates them from the rest of the town, they find themselves drawn together. As Claire helps James rediscover his passion for writing and he encourages her artistic talent, they both must confront past heartbreaks and decide if they're brave enough to follow their heart's desire.
