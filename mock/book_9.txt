Life coach and mindfulness expert Sarah Mountaineer draws on her own journey from corporate burnout to personal fulfillment to offer a practical guide for navigating life's inevitable ups and downs. Using her trademark "Mountain Method," she presents a step-by-step approach to embracing change, building resilience, and finding balance no matter what challenges arise. Through compelling case studies, research-backed strategies, and reflective exercises, readers learn how to harness the lessons from difficult times to create momentum toward their goals. Whether facing career setbacks, relationship struggles, or personal loss, Peaks and Valleys provides the tools to transform obstacles into opportunities and reach new heights of personal growth.
