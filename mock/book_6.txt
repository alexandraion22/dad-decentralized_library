In this groundbreaking exploration of the future of software development, renowned technologist Alex Programmer presents a roadmap for how artificial intelligence will transform coding practices over the next decade. From self-healing systems to collaborative AI pair programming, this book examines cutting-edge research and practical applications already emerging in the field. With interviews from leading computer scientists and case studies from innovative tech companies, Coding the Future offers both technical insights and strategic guidance for developers, engineers, and organizations preparing to navigate the rapidly evolving technological landscape.
